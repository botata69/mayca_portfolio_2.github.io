Nathalies art

Building on the user-centered design principles from Learning Outcome 1, it was crucial for my group and I to understand our target audience. Nathalie was unsure about her target audience and their needs. To ensure our rebranding efforts met these needs, my team member Shirine conducted research to gather relevant insights.

01-Research

Utilizing the DOT framework, we focused on field research by conducting a 9-question survey aimed at 15 potential users in Eindhoven. We specifically targeted furniture stores and libraries, as these locations are frequented by individuals interested in home decor and potentially art enthusiasts.
