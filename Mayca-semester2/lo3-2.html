Project - X

For Project X, I aimed to create a webshop for Bula Surf Shop Aruba. My research revealed that most users prefer to shop using mobile devices. Based on this insight, I decided to design a mobile eCommerce app to better meet the needs and preferences of the customers.

01-Moodboard

Creating a mood board helped me decide on the vibe and aesthetic for the webshop. This process allowed me to gather visual inspiration and define the overall look and feel I wanted to achieve for the Bula Surf Shop's online presence.

02-inspiration & intuition

Due to my tight deadline, I wasn't able to conduct extensive research on developing an eCommerce app. Instead, I relied on inspiration and intuition to guide my design and development process, drawing from existing successful models and my understanding of user preferences.
