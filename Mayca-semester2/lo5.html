Core Values

This learning outcome has allowed me to deeply reflect on my core values and identify what matters most to me.

Learning is a fundamental value in my life. I maintain a constant desire to learn and explore, continually educating myself and remaining open to new ideas and perspectives. This commitment to learning not only enhances my knowledge but also keeps me adaptable in an ever-changing world.

Creativity is another core value that I hold dear. It allows me to generate original ideas and engage in creative thinking. Through my designs, I express myself and bring innovative concepts to life. My journey in Media and Design has provided numerous opportunities to practice and display my creativity, showing its importance in my personal and professional development.

Compassion is the value that is closest to my heart. Compassion includes kindness, showing care and consideration for others, and being dedicated to service. It involves a willingness to give time, resources, and support to those in need. Being kind is paramount to me, and it guides my interactions and decisions. While I may not yet know exactly what kind of designer I will become, I am certain that I will pursue my career with kindness, creating work that helps others and provides a meaningful, fulfilling experience.

In summary, learning, creativity, and compassion are the core values that define me. They shape my approach to both life and work, ensuring that whatever I do, I do it with a sense of purpose, empathy, and a continuous drive for growth.
