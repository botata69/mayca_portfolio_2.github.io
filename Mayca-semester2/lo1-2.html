Bula Surf Shop currently does not have a live webshop, limiting their product visibility to in-store visitors. 01-Execution

I conducted a survey of my two target audiences: local and international Bula customers. This research aimed to gather insights on user needs, preferences, and behaviors to create a user-centric and functional webshop. Additionally, it aimed to determine if having a webshop was necessary for Bula and if customers would use it.

02-Outcome

Research revealed that most users prefer mobile devices for online shopping. Based on this finding, I focused on developing a mobile app for Bula instead of a traditional webshop. Additionally, the research indicated that both local and international customers would be interested in online shopping at Bula.

03-Interactive prototype

I developed an interactive prototype using Adobe XD, which allowed me to test and interact with it on my phone. This tool enabled me to create a realistic and functional model of the mobile app, ensuring a user-friendly design and experience. The prototype included key features such as product browsing, price viewing, and a seamless checkout process.
