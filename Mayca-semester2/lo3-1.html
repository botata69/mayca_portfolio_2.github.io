Handpicked-Breda

Handpicked tasked us with creating a marketing campaign for Explorebreda.nl, an online platform showcasing the best of Breda. Our research revealed that international students mostly use social media platforms such as instagram and tiktok. This insight alowed my team and I to focus on content creation for these specific platforms.
