About me

Hi everyone, I’m Xinamayca de Cuba, though you can call me Mayca.

This semester has been an exhilarating journey for me in
design. I’m incredibly proud of how much I’ve learned,
especially with the freedom to explore the world of UX/UI
here at Fonty's.

Outside of my design pursuits, I’m passionate about maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Exercise is a big part of my routine, and I strive to nourish my body with nutritious foods. Taking care of myself is a priority. Music is another significant aspect of my life, and I’m eager to immerse myself in the vibrant events that Europe has to offer.

When crafting my portfolio, I aimed to reflect my personality—a blend of chill vibes and positivity. It serves as a showcase for my newfound skills and represents who I am as a designer.
